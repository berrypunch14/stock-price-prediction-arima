Stock Price Prediction¶

This notebook aims to analyze and predict stock prices using time series forecasting techniques, particularly with ARIMA modeling. The dataset used includes historical stock prices for Nestlé India Limited.

Libraries used:

  • pandas, numpy for data manipulation
  • matplotlib, seaborn, and plotly for visualization
  • pmdarima for ARIMA modeling
